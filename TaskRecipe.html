WHITE SAUCE PASTA

White sauce macaroni pasta recipe explained with step by step pictures and a quick video.
This kids friendly pasta is very easy to prepare at home.
I have not used cheese in this pasta recipe.
And is prepared using raw macaroni pasta, milk, maida and vegetables.
Pasta is an Italian food and is widely adopted by Indians.
I have been preparing this pasta from 4 years.
It all happened when my son was demanding for store bought pasta, when he was 2 years old.
And I was little worried to feed him with those store bought pastas.
Then my dear friend Merin gave me this idea of making pasta at home.
I have also referred few blogs on those days.
But I seriously do not remember which blog I referred.
Anyway this pasta tastes too good, so I thought I will share it with you all.
Like my baby corn manchurian recipe, I am listing this also under kids friendly recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup macaroni pasta or any other
  • 1/2 cup chopped mix vegetables (Select from carrot, capsicum, corn, peas, cauliflower, broccoli, spring onion, cabbage and beans)
  • 1 tbsp maida or all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp pasta or pizza seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 - 3 cloves garlic
  • Salt as per your taste
